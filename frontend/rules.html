Rules & Regulations

Key laws and guidelines for citizens in Tamil Nadu.

Indian Penal Code & CrPC (Overview)

Citizens must cooperate with lawful police orders. Police must follow CrPC procedures for arrest, search, and investigation. Know your rights under Articles 21 and 22 of the Constitution.

Motor Vehicles Act

  • Always carry valid driving license, registration, and insurance.
  • Wear helmet (two-wheeler) and seatbelt (four-wheeler).
  • Do not use mobile phones while driving.
  • Pay traffic fines only through official e-challan or government counters.

Protection of Women

  • Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005
  • Sexual Harassment laws (workplace and public)
  • Zero FIR and women helpline 1091

Information Technology Act (Cybercrime)

Online fraud, harassment, and data theft are punishable. Report at cybercrime.gov.in and preserve digital evidence.

Citizen Responsibilities

  • Provide truthful information to police.
  • Do not obstruct official duty.
  • Report crimes promptly.
  • Never offer bribes — report corruption to vigilance.
